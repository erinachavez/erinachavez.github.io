What is the IQA World Cup?

The International Quidditch Association has selected the City of Florence to host the IQA World Cup, working in conjunction with Human Company. National quidditch teams from around the world, from the UK and Denmark to the USA and 2016 World Champions Australia, will fly to Florence to compete to be crowned international champions of the sport.



When will the tournament take place?

The tournament is officially from 27 June to 2 July 2018. The opening ceremony will be on Friday, with a city welcoming ceremony scheduled for Wednesday. The matches are on June 30 to July 1 2018.



What is the address of the sports venue?

The venue is in the Campo di Marte District (Viale Manfredo Fanti and Viale Pasquale Paoli, 50137 Firenze FI, Italy)



Is this a stay-and-play policy?

Yes. This means all teams are required to stay at Camping Firenze, the official hospitality venue for the event, and fans / spectators have the option to stay at the same place. You can book your stay here.

In this landing page teams and supporters will find two booking sections:

  1. Teams playing the tournament;
  2. Supporters, families and audience attending the event.


Teams playing in the tournament

Teams will find a booking form to fill in with the team’s details and request for the stay.

Teams can also indicate in the form their preferences on the accommodation type – Mobile Home Chalet and/or Bungalow Superior – and Human Company will do its best to satisfy their needs according to availability at the moment of the booking request.

Supporters, families and audience attending the event

Human Company, main partner of the IQA World Cup, offers a 10% discount on the best available online rates for all supporters, relatives and audience that will attend the event. The code for this option during booking is IQAWC2018, and it is only valid for supporters and fans.

To have the discount, simply enter the promotional code in the coupon box "Do you have a coupon / discount code" when booking online.

The accommodation options for them are:

  • PLUS Florence, our hostel and hotel in the center of Florence;
  • Firenze Camping in Town (Camping Firenze), the IQA World Cup official Village;
  • Norcenni Girasole Village, our village in Tuscany, among the Chianti hills at approx. 30 km Florence.


A chance to visit Italy

Athletes and supporters can also take advantage of IQA World Cup 2018 to visit more of Italy. Human Company offers them the opportunity to extend or anticipate their stay and visit the most famous Italian destinations such as the Chianti shire, the Tuscany Coast, Rome, Venice and the Lake Garda, staying in one of their holiday parks and city campsites with a 10% discount on the best online available rates. The discount will be available throughout June and July.

To have the discount, simply enter the promotional code in the coupon box "Do you have a coupon / discount code" when booking online.

Here below the villages available for booking before and/or after the event:

  • Altomincio Family Park > Lake Garda, Veneto Region;
  • Jolly Camping in Town > Venice, Veneto Region;
  • Park Albatros Camping Village > San Vincenzo, on the Tuscan Coast;
  • Roma Camping in Town > Rome, near the Vatican City;
  • Fabulous Camping Village > Rome, on the road to the Coast;
  • I Pini Family Park > near Rome, Fiano Romano.


For information and assistance please contact Human Company at quidditch.teams@humancompany.com or (+39) 055 4698011 (Monday to Friday, CET 9.30 a.m. to 5.00 p.m.)



Will teams be able to practice at the venue?

The IQA will be booking several fields from Wednesday - Friday so that teams may practice before the tournament weekend.
